About TECH50K

“Fifty Thousand Syrian Technicians” is a national developmental initiative presented by the Syrian British Professionals Network, aiming to build a qualified Syrian digital generation capable of innovation and competing in the global knowledge- and technology-based economy. The initiative is based on the belief that digital and programming skills are the new language of production and innovation, and that investing in Syrian human capital is essential for digital transformation and national development.

Our Vision

We strive to create a Syrian digital renaissance that empowers Syrians to be active contributors in shaping the digital future rather than mere consumers. Our vision focuses on building a broad community of Syrian technicians capable of producing technological solutions that serve development and reconstruction, competing in global markets through remote work and digital entrepreneurship, while promoting women's participation and including disadvantaged groups.

Our Mission

To deliver a comprehensive national program for digital and technical education, providing a progressive path from basic digital literacy to advanced technical skills. This enables 50,000 Syrian youth to acquire employable digital competencies, turning them into productive contributors to the local and global digital workforce, ensuring inclusivity, and empowering women.

Our Goals

  • Building a national digital skills base: Train 50,000 Syrian youth in technical fields, from basic digital literacy to professional-level expertise.
  • Enhancing digital employment and sustainable job creation: Equip beneficiaries with the technical and practical skills needed to compete locally and globally, or to start their own tech ventures.
  • Empowering Syrian women digitally and economically: Encourage women's participation in training programs and provide a supportive learning environment to enhance their professional development.
  • Establishing a sustainable national digital learning ecosystem: Develop a national digital learning platform in Arabic and English with continuously updated content and scalable opportunities.
  • Contributing to knowledge-based reconstruction and economic development: Direct technical projects to serve vital sectors such as education, healthcare, and public services, improving efficiency, quality of life, and creating a knowledge-driven economy.

Target Audience

  • Syrian youth inside Syria motivated to benefit from digital training and develop a professional path in technology, regardless of their academic background.
  • Individuals without any prior digital experience, through a foundational path that prepares them to acquire basic skills before moving to specialized technical tracks.
  • University and college graduates and students, particularly in scientific, engineering, or technical fields, seeking to enhance readiness for the digital job market or redirect their career towards modern digital fields.
  • Syrian women and girls, as core participants of the technical workforce, with supportive programs encouraging professional development and active participation in the tech sector and digital entrepreneurship.
  • Disadvantaged youth affected by conflict or limited economic opportunities, seeking to build a new career path based on digital skills and remote work.
  • Children of martyrs and those directly affected by the war, given special priority to access training and technical qualification opportunities, supporting them socially, psychologically, and professionally.
